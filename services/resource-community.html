Resource & Community

Expertise on Demand, Community by Design

Procurement transformation does not end with an implementation project. The organisations that extract the most value from their technology investments are those that maintain access to deep expertise, stay connected to industry peers, and keep pace with a rapidly evolving market.

Our Resource and Community offering provides flexible access to seasoned procurement specialists who can augment your team for specific projects or provide ongoing advisory support through retainer arrangements. Whether you need hands-on Coupa expertise, strategic guidance, or a sounding board for complex decisions, we are here when you need us.

Beyond individual support, our CPO peer network and ProcureTech intelligence services connect you with the people and insights that keep procurement leaders ahead. Through curated events, market research, and facilitated peer exchanges, we help you benchmark, learn, and grow continuously.

Talent Scarcity

Finding experienced procurement technology professionals is increasingly difficult, leaving teams under-resourced during critical transformation periods.

Knowledge Gaps

Internal teams often lack the specialised Coupa and ProcureTech knowledge needed to optimise their setup and keep up with platform releases.

Isolation from Peers

Procurement leaders often operate in silos, missing opportunities to learn from peers who have solved similar challenges in different industries.

Keeping Up with the Market

The ProcureTech landscape evolves rapidly. Without dedicated market intelligence, organisations risk falling behind on emerging capabilities and best practices.
