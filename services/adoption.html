Adoption & Support

Change management, persona-based training, post-go-live support, and continuous improvement to turn your procurement technology into a daily habit.

Adoption Services

Technology Only Delivers Value When People Use It

The most common reason procurement technology investments fail to deliver ROI is not the technology itself -- it is adoption. Even the most well-configured platform is worthless if your people do not use it consistently and correctly. Yet most implementation projects allocate less than 10% of their budget to change management and training.

Our adoption and support practice addresses this head-on. We design change management programmes that are tailored to the specific personas in your organisation, from casual requesters to strategic buyers to finance approvers. Each group gets the communication, training, and support they need to embrace the new way of working.

Beyond go-live, we provide structured support and continuous improvement services that keep adoption growing and ensure your investment compounds over time rather than decaying through disuse.

User Resistance

People naturally resist new systems and processes. Without structured change management, users find workarounds that undermine the entire investment.

Lack of Training

Generic, one-size-fits-all training sessions fail to address the unique needs and workflows of different user personas across the organisation.

No Ongoing Support

When implementation partners leave after go-live, organisations are left without the expertise to resolve issues, answer questions, and optimise their setup.

Low Adoption Metrics

Without measurement and accountability, adoption rates plateau far below the levels needed to realise the business case that justified the investment.

Our Approach

Adoption Methodology

A people-centred framework that builds momentum before go-live and sustains it long after.

Stakeholder Mapping

We identify every user persona, map their journey, assess their readiness for change, and pinpoint the champions and resistors who will shape adoption success.

Communication & Training

We develop targeted communication campaigns and role-based training programmes that speak to each persona's needs, building awareness, desire, and capability before go-live.

Go-Live Support

We provide floor-walking support, dedicated help desks, and rapid issue resolution during the critical first weeks to build user confidence and prevent workarounds from taking root.

Continuous Improvement

We monitor adoption metrics, gather user feedback, and deliver iterative improvements that keep the system evolving with your business and your people's growing capabilities.
