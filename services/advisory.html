Strategic Procurement Advisory

Technology strategy, vendor selection, procurement roadmaps, and operating model design to align your procurement technology with business objectives.

Advisory Services

Navigate the Procurement Technology Landscape

The procurement technology market has grown exponentially, with hundreds of solutions competing for attention. Choosing the right platform, building a compelling business case, and designing an operating model that delivers lasting value requires deep domain expertise and an unbiased perspective.

Our advisory practice brings over 20 years of practitioner experience to help you cut through vendor noise and make technology decisions grounded in your business reality. We work alongside your leadership team to develop strategies that are actionable, stakeholder-aligned, and designed for measurable ROI.

Whether you are evaluating your first procurement platform or optimising an existing landscape, our advisory engagements give you the clarity and confidence to move forward with purpose.

Fragmented Vendor Landscape

Hundreds of overlapping procurement tools make it nearly impossible to identify the right-fit solution without expert guidance and structured evaluation.

Unclear ROI

Without a rigorous business case methodology, procurement technology investments struggle to gain executive sponsorship and board approval.

Misaligned Tech & Business Strategy

Technology decisions made in isolation from business process needs lead to costly rework, low adoption, and unrealised benefits.

Budget Constraints

Procurement technology budgets are under constant scrutiny. Without a clear value story, investments get deprioritised or cancelled entirely.

Our Approach

Advisory Methodology

A proven four-step framework that takes you from current-state assessment to an actionable, stakeholder-approved roadmap.

Discovery & Assessment

We assess your current procurement maturity, technology landscape, pain points, and strategic ambitions through stakeholder interviews, process mapping, and data analysis.

Strategy Development

We define your target operating model, identify capability gaps, and build a strategic vision that connects procurement technology investments to business outcomes.

Vendor Evaluation

We run a structured, unbiased vendor evaluation using weighted scoring criteria aligned to your requirements, including demonstrations, reference checks, and total cost of ownership analysis.

Roadmap Delivery

We deliver a prioritised, phased technology roadmap with a robust business case, governance framework, and stakeholder communication plan ready for executive sign-off.
