Implementation & Configuration

Business process-first Coupa implementation, iterative delivery, seamless integration, and thorough testing for production-ready results.

Implementation Services

Built on Business Process, Not Just Technology

Too many procurement implementations fail because they lead with technology configuration rather than business process design. At DPNow, we take a fundamentally different approach: we start with your processes, your people, and your objectives, then configure Coupa to support them.

Our implementation methodology is iterative and collaborative. We work in structured sprints, validating each build increment with your business users before moving forward. This reduces rework, catches issues early, and ensures the final solution genuinely reflects how your teams operate.

With over 50 Coupa implementations behind us, we bring deep platform expertise combined with real-world procurement knowledge. We handle end-to-end delivery including integration design, data migration, testing, and deployment, so you can focus on preparing your organisation for change.

Scope Creep

Poorly defined requirements and shifting priorities cause timelines and budgets to spiral out of control during implementation projects.

Business Process Gaps

Configuring technology without first mapping and optimising business processes leads to a system that nobody wants to use.

Integration Complexity

Connecting procurement platforms to ERP, finance, and master data systems requires deep technical knowledge and careful orchestration.

Testing Inadequacy

Insufficient testing coverage means critical defects slip into production, eroding user confidence and requiring costly post-go-live fixes.

Our Approach

Implementation Methodology

A structured, iterative framework that delivers quality at every phase, from blueprint to hypercare.

Blueprint & Design

We document your business processes, define solution architecture, map integrations, and create a detailed design blueprint that serves as the single source of truth for the entire project.

Configuration & Build

We configure Coupa iteratively in structured sprints, validating each module with business users. Integration development and data migration run in parallel to keep the project on track.

Testing & Validation

We execute comprehensive testing across unit, integration, end-to-end, and user acceptance phases. Every test scenario is traced back to business requirements to ensure complete coverage.

Deployment & Hypercare

We manage a controlled go-live with detailed cutover planning, followed by an intensive hypercare period where our team provides hands-on support to resolve issues rapidly.
