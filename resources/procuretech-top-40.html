Our curated ranking of the most impactful procurement technology solutions in the market.

About the Ranking

The ProcureTech Top 40 is DPNow's annual assessment of the procurement technology landscape. Based on our hands-on experience with dozens of platforms, client feedback, and market analysis, we rank the solutions that are making the biggest impact on procurement operations.

Our evaluation considers: platform breadth, ease of implementation, user adoption rates, innovation pipeline, customer satisfaction, and total cost of ownership.
