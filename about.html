Our Story

Born from Real Procurement Experience

Digital Procurement Now was founded by Nico Bac after more than 20 years leading procurement transformation at Procter & Gamble. Having lived the complexity of enterprise procurement firsthand, Nico set out to build a firm that truly understands what procurement leaders face every day.

Our mission is simple: make digital procurement accessible and valuable for every organization. We combine deep practitioner experience with technology expertise to deliver outcomes that matter — real adoption, real savings, real transformation.

With our strategy hub in Geneva, Switzerland and our delivery center in Bucharest, Romania, we serve clients across Europe and beyond. Our deep Coupa partnership and Source-to-Pay expertise mean we bring both strategic vision and hands-on implementation capability to every engagement.
